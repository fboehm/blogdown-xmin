Overview

I’ve been reading Knott and Haley (2000), an article that discusses methods for multivariate QTL mapping. I’m trying to pay special attention to their methods for testing the competing hypotheses of 1. close linkage and 2. pleiotropy for a pair of traits that map to a single genomic region.

References

Knott, Sara A, and Chris S Haley. 2000. “Multitrait Least Squares for Quantitative Trait Loci Detection.” Genetics 156 (2). Genetics Soc America: 899–911.
